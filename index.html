Projects

Universal Motorcycle Central Stand

What: An innovative, user-friendly central stand for motorcycles based on a Slider-Crank Mechanism, designed to reduce deployment effort. Patent filed (App No: 202541004656 A, Journal No: 07/2025, Dated: 14/02/2025).

How: Developed using SolidWorks for design. Conducted stress analysis using SOLIDWORKS structural simulation and validated with hand calculations.

Why: To enhance the usability of motorcycle stands, making them more accessible and easier to operate for a wider range of users, thereby improving the overall user experience.

EV Powertrain Efficiency Optimization

What: A mathematical model to simulate and optimize the efficiency of an electric vehicle's power system.

How: Developed in Matlab to analyze and improve energy consumption (Wh/Km).

Why: To contribute to the advancement of sustainable transportation by enhancing the performance, reducing energy consumption, and extending the range of electric vehicles.

Electronics Cooling (Conjugate Heat Transfer)

What: Analysis of simultaneous heat transfer in solid and fluid domains for cooling multiple electronic chips.

How: Utilized Ansys Fluent to simulate and analyze heat transfer at the solid-fluid interface.

Why: To develop more effective and reliable cooling solutions for electronic components, ensuring their optimal performance, longevity, and preventing overheating.

Battery Thermal Management for EVs

What: Analysis of nanofluid thermal behavior for maintaining temperature uniformity and reducing maximum battery temperature in EVs.

How: Used Ansys Fluent to analyze nanofluid thermal properties in a thermal network design.

Why: To improve the safety, performance, and lifespan of electric vehicle batteries through advanced thermal management, preventing degradation and ensuring operational stability.
